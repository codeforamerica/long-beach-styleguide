    Why the Reading Grade Level of your content is important to consider
  • 8th grade is the average reading grade level in America.
  • The simpler the sentence structure, the better the translation will be through the Google Translate algorithm.

      • To have public facing written material be accessible to the widest population, we recommend using the free service HemingwayApp.com You can copy your text into the website and it will identify the reading grade level as well as specific ways to make it more accessible.
